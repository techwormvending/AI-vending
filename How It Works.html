Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if I accidentally take the wrong item?
No problem! Just put it back before closing the door. Our AI will recognize that you returned it and won't charge you for it.
Q: How long does the whole process take?
From scanning the QR code to walking away with your items typically takes less than 30 seconds. First-time setup takes about 60 seconds.
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards. Your payment information is securely stored and encrypted for fast, safe transactions.
Q: Can I see my purchase history?
Absolutely! All your transactions are stored in your account and you'll receive a digital receipt via WhatsApp after each purchase.
Q: What if the AI makes a mistake?
Our system has 99.9% accuracy, but if there's ever an issue, simply contact us via WhatsApp and we'll resolve it immediately with a full refund if needed.
Q: Is my personal information safe?
Yes! We use bank-level encryption and are fully PCI-compliant. Your data is secure and we never share your information with third parties.
Q: Do I need to download an app?
Nope! Everything works through WhatsApp, which you probably already have. No additional apps to download or accounts to create.
Q: What happens if I can't access WhatsApp?
While WhatsApp is our primary interface, you can also call us at (412) 779-9809 for assistance or alternative access methods.
