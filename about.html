Our Story

Techworm is revolutionizing the way people access snacks and refreshments. We've replaced outdated retail solutions with smart, AI-powered refrigerators that make getting what you need fast, easy, and hassle-free.

Our system is simple: insert or tap your card, open the door, take what you want, and go. Our AI automatically detects what you took and charges you accordingly. No buttons to press, no products getting stuck, no waiting in line.

Starting in Pittsburgh with our first location in a music studio, we're proving that retail can be better. We're expanding to offices, gyms, creative spaces, and anywhere people need convenient access to refreshments.
