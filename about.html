Our Story

Techworm Vending was born from a simple observation: traditional vending machines are stuck in the past. We saw people fumbling with coins, dealing with jammed machines, and missing out on the convenience that modern technology can provide.

Starting with a single smart refrigerator in a music studio in Pittsburgh, we set out to prove that vending could be different. By combining artificial intelligence with a trust-based approach, we've created an experience that's as simple as opening your own fridge at home.

Our first machine serves creative professionals who need quick access to refreshments during long recording sessions. No interruptions, no hassle ‚Äì just grab what you need and keep creating. The response has been incredible, and we're excited to bring this innovation to more locations.
