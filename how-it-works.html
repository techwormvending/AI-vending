Frequently Asked Questions

Q: What if I accidentally take the wrong item?
No problem! Just put it back before closing the door. Our AI will recognize that you returned it and won't charge you for it.
Q: How long does the whole process take?
From inserting your card to walking away with your items typically takes less than 30 seconds. It's faster than traditional vending!
Q: What payment methods do you accept?
We accept all major credit and debit cards, including contactless tap-to-pay. Your payment information is securely encrypted.
Q: How do I get my receipt?
After closing the door, a QR code appears on the screen. Scan it with your phone camera to receive your digital receipt. This is optional - you're charged automatically whether you scan it or not.
Q: What if the AI makes a mistake?
Our system has 99.9% accuracy, but if there's ever an issue, simply contact us at (412) 779-9809 and we'll resolve it immediately with a full refund if needed.
Q: Do I need an app or account?
Nope! Just insert or tap your card and you're ready to go. No apps to download, no accounts to create.
Q: Is my payment information safe?
Yes! We use bank-level encryption and are fully PCI-compliant. Your payment data is secure and never stored on our machines.
Q: What if I need help?
Contact us anytime at (412) 779-9809, via WhatsApp, or email sales@techwormvending.com. We're here to help!
